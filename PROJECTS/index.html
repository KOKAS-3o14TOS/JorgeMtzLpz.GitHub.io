VIDEOGAME-MR.KOKAS

This is the first video game I made in 2020. Mr.kokas is a game about to kills zombies until they have already eaten you. The logic system has a reputation system in that the winner saves his points, and always is the best score.

SMART-SHIPPING

It is a prototype of an intelligent container, which measures in real time the temperature and humidity of the environment to collect them in a database and to be able to make predictions and monitor the quality of perishables. Our goal is to apply intelligent systems to shipping containers in order to transport foodstuffs such as avocados to new European and Asian industries.

PLANTPAL

Plantpal is an agriculture 4.0 project, which allowed the development of a robot to improve production processes by making them faster, more economical and sustainable, and to help farmers make better decisions about their products. It is a robot remotely controlled from a web page and in that same web page it shows in real time the different samples of the environment (soil and ground humidity, and temperature).

ECOPLAY

This game is the first game that I sold. Ecoplay is a game that is racing. The principal focus is promoting the different materials that they could be recycled, in groups of around 6 and 10 age. The system is to do the best time to finish racing, if you get a recycle thing your time is less.
